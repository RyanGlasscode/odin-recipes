Lunchtime Sandwich!

A staple of any parents lunch routine for kids!

Ham and cheese sandwich!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Butter
  • Bread white

Lets cook!...or assemble

  1. Take 2 pieces of bread and butter both.
  2. Take ham and a handful of cheese and place on sandwich.
  3. Close the sandwich and eat up!.
